THE IMPORTANCE OF SUNDARBAN

— The Natural Shield of Bangladesh —

Location & Status

  • World's largest mangrove forest.
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • South-western delta of Bangladesh.

Ecological Benefits

  • Massive Carbon sequestration.
  • Shields against Cyclone Sidr & Aila.
  • Maintains regional climate balance.

Biodiversity

Home to the Royal Bengal Tiger, spotted deer, crocodiles, and thousands of unique plant species like Sundari & Gewa.

Economic Value

Livelihood for millions through fishing, honey collection (Mawals), and sustainable tourism.
